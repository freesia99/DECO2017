Research
Do we need primary and secondary research or is secondary fine?

We always encourage you to do interviews and surveys, in order to get a better understanding of your users and their needs. However, it is not a requirement for this assessment and we are not evaluating your ability to do primary research. We are evaluating the profiles themselves and how much depth and useful information they have, which would likely come about from combining insights from multiple research methods.

How many personas should we be doing?

1 is the minimum required amount, although it is very likely there are groups of users, who all have different characteristics and needs.
